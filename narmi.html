Narmi Banking Alexa Skill

At Georgetown's Hoya Hacks hackacthon we partnered with Narmi (a company that enables financial institutions to deliver their own digital services) to create an Alexa skill for their new banking API.

We produced a skill that enabled the user to ask questions using natural language to access important banking features. For example:

"What is my current balance?"

"Transfer money from my primary account to my secondary account."

"What is my transaction history?"
